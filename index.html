Welcome to mooofin's Homepage

Hi, I'm Siddharth.

I build systems tools, reverse engineering writeups, and low-level projects.

I enjoy NixOS, Gentoo, and declarative workflows with Nix.

Currently working on NoVMP — a fork of can1357's static devirtualizer for VMProtect x64 3.x. The original backend targets VTIL-Core, which is abandoned and never had a working compiler. I'm ripping it out and replacing it with an LLVM IR backend: VMP handler emulation and IL lifting from the original frontend stay intact, the optimization and recompilation pipeline gets rebuilt on top of llvm::Module and standard IR passes. WIP, not yet functional.
